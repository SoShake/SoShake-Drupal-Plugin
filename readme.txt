
-- SUMMARY --

With our SoShake WordPress Plugin you can easily insert all social sharing options in your website, so all your readers can share your content to their network.

We also make your website Social Friendly by adding all the Open Graph data so your website can be perfetcly shared and knowned on any social network such as Facebook.

-- REQUIREMENTS --

None.


-- INSTALLATION --

* Install as usual, see http://drupal.org/node/70151 for further information.
